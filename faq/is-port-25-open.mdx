## Does EDIS filter or block any ports?

**EDIS doesn't have any ports filtered or closed.** Public IP addresses are configured directly on your VPS network interface. You decide which services to use and which firewall rules you wish to configure. From our side, there are no limits.

  lead to account termination without eligibility for a refund.
</Note>
