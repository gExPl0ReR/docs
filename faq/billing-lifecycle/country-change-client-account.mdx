## Country Changes in Client Accounts

For accounting, tax, and regulatory compliance reasons, we generally **do not allow changes to the country associated with a client account**.

The country assigned to an account determines:

- Applicable **VAT rules**
- **Invoicing requirements**
- **Tax reporting obligations**

Changing the country may affect **past and future invoices**, which is why such changes are restricted.

---

## When Can a Country Change Be Considered?

A country change may only be reviewed if **both** of the following conditions are met:

- The **current country is outside the European Union**
- The **new country is also outside the European Union**

<Note>
  Country updates may only be considered for **non-EU → non-EU** changes.
</Note>

---

## When Country Changes Are Not Allowed

Country changes are **not permitted if any EU country is involved**, including:

- Moving **from a non-EU country to an EU country**
- Moving **from an EU country to a non-EU country**
- Moving **between two EU countries**

<Warning>
  If any **EU country** is involved in the change, the country in the existing account **cannot be updated**.
</Warning>

---

## If Your Legal Country Has Changed

If your **legal residence** or **company registration** has changed to a country that involves the **European Union**, a **new client account must be created** under the correct country.

<Note>
  Services cannot be transferred between client accounts.
</Note>

## Updating Other Account Details

You can update other account details at any time in your client area, including:

- Company Name
- Address
- Email address
- VAT ID

Update them here:

[Open Control Panel](https://manage.edisglobal.com/clientarea.php?action=details)

---

## Requesting a Non-EU → Non-EU Country Update

If you believe your case qualifies for a **non-EU → non-EU country update**, please contact:

[**support@edisglobal.com**](mailto:support@edisglobal.com)

Our team will review the request and confirm whether the change can be applied.

  Moving or transferring existing VPS services to a different client account is **not possible**.
