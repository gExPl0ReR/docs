## Build a Reliable, Low-Maintenance Billing Workflow

At EDIS Global, we’ve worked with **tens of thousands of customers over more than two decades** — from single VPS users to large-scale infrastructures.

This guide explains how to set up a **reliable, fully automated billing workflow** that minimizes manual work, avoids service interruptions, and keeps everything running smoothly.

---

## Recommended Billing Methods

EDIS Global supports multiple payment methods. Some are ideal for full automation, while others require a bit of planning.

---

## Credit Cards (Recommended)

**Automation level:** ⭐⭐⭐\
**Reliability:** ⭐⭐⭐\
**Operational overhead:** ⭐ (lowest)

Credit cards offer the **most reliable and maintenance-free billing experience**.

When you pay by credit card:

- Your card is securely stored with our payment provider **Stripe**
- Only a reference token is stored in your account
- Invoices are charged automatically when due

### Benefits

- Renewal invoices are charged automatically
- New orders are charged instantly
- Services are provisioned in **near real-time**

**Best for:**\
Customers who want fully automated billing with immediate service delivery.

---

## Account Credit (Highly Recommended)

**Automation level:** ⭐⭐⭐\
**Reliability:** ⭐⭐⭐\
**Operational overhead:** ⭐⭐

Account credit is a powerful option, especially for customers with multiple services.

By topping up your account:

- Renewal invoices are paid automatically from your balance
- New orders are funded instantly
- Services are delivered in near real-time

### Important

You only need to ensure that **sufficient credit is available** in your account.

### How to Fund Account Credit

- Credit card
- PayPal
- SEPA direct debit or Bank Transfer
- Cryptocurrency payments

**Best for:**\
Businesses, resellers, or customers who prefer prepaid billing and predictable costs.

---

## SEPA Direct Debit

**Automation level:** ⭐⭐\
**Reliability:** ⭐⭐\
**Operational overhead:** ⭐⭐⭐

SEPA direct debit allows invoices to be debited automatically from your bank account.

### What to Know

- Funds usually settle **4–5 business days** (secure renewal is guaranteed)
- New services are delivered **after settlement**
- Not ideal if you require immediate provisioning
- Seettlement is delayed by public holidays and weekends

### Recommended Use

SEPA direct debit works best when used to **top up account credit**.\
This combines automated billing with instant service delivery.

We can assist with setting up the mandate and migrating your services smoothly to SEPA Direct Debit.

## PayPal Subscriptions

**Automation level:** ⭐\
**Reliability:** ⭐\
**Operational overhead:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

PayPal subscriptions can work for:

- Private users
- A small number of static services

However, they can become difficult to manage for business use cases involving frequent changes (upgrades, cancellation + re-orders)

**Recommendation:**\
For business customers, we strongly recommend **credit cards or account credit** instead.

For a smooth, fully automated billing experience, choose one of the following setups:

- Credit card billing
- Account credit funded by credit card
- Account credit funded by SEPA direct debit, Bank Transfer or Crypto

These setups consistently result in fewer billing issues and uninterrupted service.
