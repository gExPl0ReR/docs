## How to Locate and Cancel a Subscription in Your PayPal Account

### Introduction

This article provides a step-by-step guide to help you locate and cancel a subscription in your PayPal account. Follow these instructions to manage your recurring payments effectively.&#x20;

### Step-by-Step Guide

###

### 1. Log in to PayPal

Go to the [PayPal website](https://www.paypal.com) and log in with your account credentials.

### 2. Access Account Settings

Click on the gear icon located in the top right corner to open your account settings.

### 3. Navigate to Payments

In the settings menu, click on the "Payments" tab to view your payment settings.

### 4. Manage Automatic Payments

Under the "Automatic payments" section, click on "Manage automatic payments" to view your active subscriptions.

### 5. Select the Subscription

From the list of active subscriptions, select the one you want to cancel.

### 6. Cancel the Subscription

Click on the subscription details and choose "Cancel" to stop the recurring payment.

### Conclusion

By following these steps, you can easily locate and cancel any subscriptions in your PayPal account. For further assistance, visit the [PayPal Help Center](https://www.paypal.com/us/smarthelp/home).
