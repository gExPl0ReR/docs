## Pay your invoice in another currency (with PayPal)

Follow these simple steps to make a payment in EUR, when your PayPal balance is in USD or any other currency.

1. Log in to your PayPal account

2. Click on "balance"

3. Manage currencies

4. Select EUR as your "primary" currency

5. Make the payment

6. Revert settings back to your previous currency
