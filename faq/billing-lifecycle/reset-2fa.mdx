## Lost access to your 2FA device?

If you no longer have access to your authenticator app and backup codes, our support team can help you regain access to your account.

---

## What you need to provide

To verify account ownership, please contact our support team from your **registered email address** by email and include the following information:

- **Registered account email address**
- **One of the following:**
  - an IP address of an active VPS in your account
  - a recent invoice number or invoice amount
- **Payment method used** (e.g. PayPal, bank transfer, crypto)

---

## Additional verification (for security)

For security reasons, we may require one or more of the following:

- **Payment reference details**\
  (e.g. PayPal transaction ID, bank reference, or crypto TXID)
- **Billing details**\
  (e.g. billing name or company name associated with the account)
- **Email confirmation**\
  You may be asked to confirm your request via a verification link sent to your registered email address.

---

## What happens next

Once your request is verified:

- 2FA will be temporarily disabled on your account
- you will be able to log in using your password
- you can then re-enable 2FA

---

## Important

- Requests must be made from the **registered email address**
- You must still know your **account password**
- For security reasons, additional verification may be required
- In some cases, a short **security review period** may apply before 2FA is reset

---

## Tip

To avoid losing access in the future:

- store your backup codes securely
- use a password manager
- consider using multiple devices for your 2FA app
