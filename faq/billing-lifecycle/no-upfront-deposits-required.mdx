## Guide for new customers

### Do I need to deposit funds to place an order?

<Note>
  **No, you don’t need to deposit funds** to start using our service! Simply
  place your order using **Credit Card, PayPal, or Cryptocurrency** at checkout.
</Note>

### **Tips for easier payments:**

If you anticipate making frequent small payments or if you plan to be absent for a while, consider topping up your account. This can help avoid transaction fees and ensure your invoices are paid automatically from available account credit.
