## **How Fast Do You Deliver My VPS Order?**

EDIS Global wants to ensure a great experience for all of its customers.  In order to successfully welcome new clients, there are some essential checks that need to be completed.

### **Instant VPS Deployment**

**VPS servers are deployed instantly ⚡ after payment confirmation.**

Linux systems are typically ready within **5 minutes**, while Windows deployments may take up to 15 minutes to install.

For crypto payments, provisioning begins once the required **blockchain confirmations** are completed. You will receive a payment confirmation once this process is finished.

**Provisioning starts immediately after payment is confirmed.**

​	•	**PayPal & Credit Cards**: **near-realtime ⚡**

​	•	**Crypto Payments**: Subject to blockchain confirmations

Once **payment clears**, your server is **delivered instantly**.

### When things take longer than expected

In some rare occasions, we can't deliver your order right away. It can be a temporary resource-shortage, or the need to fulfill further compliance checks. EDIS Global team would reach out to you for additional information (if required), or offer a refund.
