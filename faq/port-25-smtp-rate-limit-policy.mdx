# Is Port 25 Open for Email?

At EDIS Global, we understand the importance of email services for our customers. While **port 25 is open** for outbound SMTP traffic, we implement a **rate limit of 8 packets per second (pps)** to ensure fair usage and protect the integrity of our network.

This limitation is sufficient for **regular email communication** and does not affect legitimate email-sending activities. Our rate-limiting policy is designed to prevent abuse while allowing normal email traffic to flow seamlessly.
