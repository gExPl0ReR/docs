### Senior Linux Systems Engineer (KVM & Hosting Expert)

### About EDIS Global:

EDIS Global is a leading and fast-growing company in the VPS hosting market, operating across 50 data centers worldwide. We provide robust and high-performance hosting solutions to an international clientele. With a strong commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we pride ourselves on being a key player in the hosting industry, driven by a team of passionate IT professionals.

### Job Overview:

We are looking for a highly experienced **Senior Linux Systems Engineer (m/f/d)** with deep expertise in Linux operating systems and extensive hands-on experience in **KVM (libvirt/qemu)** environments. The ideal candidate has a strong background in the hosting industry, a passion for system monitoring, and thrives in high-pressure situations.

In this role, you'll be working with a **multi-national team**, managing servers across **50 data centers** globally, and taking on the responsibility of keeping our infrastructure running smoothly 24/7. Flexible work hours, including **on-call duties during nights and weekends**, are an integral part of the job. If you love coffee and working with a team of enthusiastic IT professionals, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

### Key Responsibilities:

- **Linux Systems Management**: Manage, maintain, and troubleshoot Linux-based servers and environments with a focus on stability, performance, and security.
- **KVM Virtualization**: Oversee and optimize virtual environments using **KVM (libvirt/qemu)**, ensuring efficient resource utilization and high availability.
- **System Monitoring & Troubleshooting**: Monitor system performance, identify potential issues, and troubleshoot critical incidents to minimize downtime.
- **Hands-On and On-Call**: Be prepared for hands-on troubleshooting and on-call duties during nights and weekends to handle emergencies and ensure 24/7 uptime.
- **Collaboration with Data Centers**: Coordinate with remote teams and **50 global data centers** to ensure proper setup, maintenance, and connectivity of systems.
- **Multi-Project Handling**: Juggle multiple tasks and projects simultaneously, ensuring timely completion and high standards of quality.
- **Automation & Optimization**: Develop and implement automation scripts and tools to optimize system performance and streamline operations.
- **Documentation & Knowledge Sharing**: Document solutions and best practices to improve team knowledge and workflow efficiency.

### Requirements:

- **Linux Expertise**: Extensive hands-on experience in Linux operating systems (preferably over several years), including server setup, maintenance, and performance tuning.
- **KVM Virtualization**: In-depth experience with **KVM (libvirt/qemu)** in production environments, including virtual machine deployment, configuration, and troubleshooting.
- **Hosting Industry Background**: Proven experience working with a hosting provider or similar environment, managing large-scale infrastructure.
- **System Monitoring**: Strong experience with monitoring tools like Prometheus, Nagios, Zabbix, or similar, and a proactive approach to identifying and resolving issues.
- **On-Call Availability**: Willingness to be on-call during nights and weekends to handle emergencies and maintain 24/7 uptime.
- **Multi-Tasking Skills**: Ability to manage multiple projects, prioritize effectively, and stay calm under pressure.
- **Communication Skills**: Excellent communication skills to collaborate with a multi-national team and data centers across the globe.
- **Stress-Resistant**: Comfortable handling high-pressure situations, solving critical issues, and keeping systems running smoothly.

### Nice to Have:

- **Scripting & Automation**: Experience with automation tools like Ansible, Puppet, or scripting languages such as Bash, Python, etc.
- **Storage & Networking Knowledge**: Familiarity with SAN, NAS, and IP networking protocols.
- **Experience with Hosting Panels**: Knowledge of hosting control panels such as cPanel, Plesk, or Webmin is a plus.
- **Virtual Coffee Lover**: A passion for coffee (virtual or real) and a sense of humor to keep up with a team of **crazy IT people**.

### Work Environment:

- **Remote Work Opportunity**: Flexible work hours with a remote working setup.
- **International Team**: Work closely with a diverse, multi-national team spread across different time zones.
- **Dynamic Projects**: Be part of multiple exciting projects in a fast-paced environment.
- **Team Collaboration**: Regular communication and collaboration using tools like Slack, Asana, and Intercom.
- **Continuous Learning**: An environment that fosters continuous learning and professional growth.
