## Product Updates

Updates and improvements

<Update label="January 2026">
  ### January 12, 2026 — Smarter 90% Traffic Warnings for Auto-Hydration 🚦📊

  Previously, **90% traffic usage warnings** were sent every time a trigger threshold was reached — even when **Auto-Hydration** was enabled.

  For customers with **high traffic consumption**, this could result in repeated warning emails shortly after each automatic traffic assignment, despite having **sufficient traffic pool balance** and no risk of throttling.

  **Improvement**

  Starting **January 12, 2026**, we no longer send **90% traffic limit warning emails** when:

  - **Auto-Hydration is enabled**, and  
  - the account has **enough traffic pool balance** to cover the next automatic hydration.

  This ensures traffic warnings are only sent when **action is actually required**, reducing unnecessary notifications while keeping customers fully informed when there is a real risk of traffic throttling.
</Update>

<Update label="December 2025">
  ### December 28, 2025 — Due-Date Reminders for Partially Paid Invoices 💳⏰

  EDIS Global applies **account credit automatically during billrun** (unless you’ve opted out of automatic credit application).\
  If the available credit is **not sufficient**, the invoice is **partially paid** and remains **unpaid** until the outstanding amount is settled.

  Starting **December 28, 2025**, we send **payment reminders at the service due date** for invoices that:

  - received a **partial credit payment**, and
  - still have an **outstanding amount due**.

  This helps ensure important renewals don’t get missed when only a small remainder are left to pay.
</Update>

<Update label="September 2025">
  ### September 25, 2025 — Post-Install Scripts Deprecated ⚠️➡️ Cloud-Init Only

  We’ve officially **retired Post-Install Scripts** from the EDIS Global VPS platform.\
  All new operating system installations now rely exclusively on **Cloud-Init**, the industry standard for automated server initialization.

  👉 Why this change?

  - **Unified automation**: Cloud-Init replaces custom Post-Install scripts with a standardized, more powerful configuration method.
  - **Faster deployments**: Inject configs directly at installation — no need for extra script execution afterwards.
  - **Broader compatibility**: Supported across nearly all Linux distributions we offer.
  - **Future-ready**: Easier scaling, improved security, and cleaner workflows.

  If you’ve used Post-Install Scripts before, don’t worry — we’ve kept the [archived documentation](https://docs.edisglobal.com/vps-management/post-install-scripts) for reference.\
  For all current and future VPS setups, please use our [Cloud-Init Installation Guide](https://docs.edisglobal.com/vps-management/edis-cloud-init-vps-installation).
</Update>
