# Subscribe to Status Updates

Stay informed about **incidents, maintenance, and service status** for the locations and services you use.

You can subscribe to updates via:

- Email
- Slack
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Chat

You can also choose **specific locations only**, so you avoid notifications from regions you don't use.

<Note>
You only receive notifications for locations where the **bell icon is enabled**.
</Note>

---

## Quick Overview

Subscribing takes less than a minute.

1. Click **Subscribe**
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Click the **magic link** in the email
4. Select the **locations you want to follow**
5. Choose your **notification method**
6. Click **Save Changes**

---

## 1. Open the Status Page

Go to the EDIS Global Statuspage:

https://status.edis.global

This page shows the **current operational status and uptime metrics** for all EDIS Global services.

---

## 2. Click **Subscribe**

In the top right corner of the page, click the **Subscribe** button.

---

## 3. Enter Your Email Address

A dialog will appear asking for your email address.

Enter the address where you want to receive notifications and click:

**Start subscription**

---

## 4. Confirm via Magic Link

You will receive an email containing a **Magic Link**.

Click:

**Sign in to EDIS Global Status | EDBB Status**

This link authenticates you and opens your subscription settings.

---

## 5. Select Locations or Services

Once signed in, you can choose which **regions or services** you want to follow.

Examples include:

- **KVM Europe**
- **KVM Asia, Australia and Oceania**
- **KVM North and Latin America**
- **KVM Middle East and Africa**

---

## 6. Subscribe to Specific Locations

Expand a region to see individual locations.

Click the **bell icon 🔔** next to the locations you want to follow.

Examples:

- Austria, Vienna  
- Germany, Frankfurt  
- France, Paris  
- Greece, Thessaloniki  

### Icon Meaning

| Icon | Meaning |
|-----|--------|
| 🔔 | Notifications enabled |
| 🔕 | Notifications disabled |

---

## 7. Choose Your Notification Method

You can choose **how you want to receive notifications**.

In the **top-right corner**, click:

**Preferences**

Then enable the notification methods you prefer.

Available options:

- **Email**
- **Slack**
- **Microsoft Teams**
- **Google Chat**

You can enable **multiple methods at the same time**.

---

## 8. Save Your Changes

After selecting your locations and notification methods, click:

**Save Changes**

Your notification preferences are now active.

---

## Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time.

Return to the status page and:

- disable the **bell icon** for specific locations  
- or choose **Unsubscribe completely**

---

## Status API

For monitoring systems and automation, the **EDIS Global Status API** provides programmatic access to service health and incident data.

The API allows you to retrieve:

- overall service status
- component health
- uptime metrics
- incident history
- maintenance events

API endpoint:

https://status.edis.global/api

Rate limit: **10 requests per second**
