### What's Next?

Please keep an eye on your mailbox for an important email from [**support@edisglobal.com**](mailto:support@edisglobal.com). This email contains everything you need to get started with your Virtual Private Server (VPS).

### What's in the Email?

1. **Login Details**: Your unique username and password to access your VPS.

2. **Server IP Addresses**: Both your IPv4 address and IPv6 address range.

3. **Management Portal**: A direct link to the <a href="https://manage.edisglobal.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank">Management Portal</a> where you can manage your VPS settings and more.

4. **Knowledge Base Access**: Get help anytime with the <a href="https://docs.edisglobal.com" target="_blank">EDIS Global Knowledgebase</a>, filled with tips and solutions to common questions.

5. **Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)**: A link to our <a href="https://docs.edisglobal.com/acceptable-use-policy" target="_blank">AUP</a> so you're familiar with our operational guidelines.

6. **Status Page**: Stay up-to-date with server status, maintenance schedules, and uptime via our <a href="https://status.edis.global" target="_blank">Status Page</a>.

7. **Support Information**: Need help? You'll find details on how to contact the <a href="https://www.edisglobal.com/contact" target="_blank">EDIS Global Support Team</a> anytime.

### **No Email Received?**

If the email hasn't arrived, please [click here](https://docs.edisglobal.com/email-with-password-not-received-solution) for help. We'll make sure everything's in order!
