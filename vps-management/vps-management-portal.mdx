## **Overview EDIS KVM management portal**

Here is a short list of available features of our <a href="https://manage.edisglobal.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank">KVM Management portal</a>.

- [Powercycle](./power-cycle-or-reboot-a-vps), Reset, explicit Power-OFF/ON
- [1-click Installer](./autoinstall-reinstall-install-vps-linux-windows)
  of common Linux distributions CentOS 9, CentOS 8, CentOS 7 Debian 12 (Bookworm),
  Debian 11 (Bullseye), Debian 10 (Buster), Debian 9 (Stretch) Ubuntu 22.04, Ubuntu
  18.04, Ubuntu 16.04
- [1-click Installer](./autoinstall-reinstall-install-vps-linux-windows) for Windows Server 2022, 2019, 2016, Windows 10
- 100+ free and ready-to-use [Turnkey system images](./autoinstall-reinstall-install-vps-linux-windows)
- [Bring your own ISO](./bring-your-own-iso) (we are cloud-init ready) or mount prefab ISOs
- [rDNS PTR](../faq/ip-addresses/setting-up-reverse-pointers) delegation for IPv4 and IPv6
- [VNC server](./enable-vnc-server) including a noVNC web client for rescue works
- Access to special KVM "hardware settings" (ethernet, display, cores vs sockets)
- [Post-Install Scripts](./post-install-scripts) (automate your deployment)
- CPU statistics
- [Traffic information](../faq/traffic-bandwidth/traffic-stats-refill-explained) traffic/bandwidth, reset, refill
- [Traffic pool](../faq/traffic-bandwidth/traffic-pool-global-and-premium-traffic-zone) how it works, traffic zones, how to buy traffic, assign traffic, auto-refill
- [KVM Rest API](../api-reference2/) for each and every function
- Your servers' [Support PIN](./your-support-pin)
