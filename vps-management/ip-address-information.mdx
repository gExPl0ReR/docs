## IP settings, Network Info

You can view and manage your VPS's IP address settings in the KVM management portal. This is where you will find the IPv4 and IPv6 addresses, gateway, subnet, and DNS servers.

<Steps>
  <Step title='Log in to your'>
    [VPS Management Portal](https://manage.edisglobal.com/clientarea.php)
  </Step>
  <Step title='Locate your VPS in the Services tab' />
  <Step title='Click "Network info"' />
</Steps>
