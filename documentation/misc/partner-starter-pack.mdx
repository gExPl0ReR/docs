### Traffic Pool Model (Additional Bandwidth)

In addition to per‚ÄëVPS included bandwidth, EDIS Global offers a **Traffic Pool** system:

- Shared traffic pool across all services in your account
- Traffic can be assigned manually or automatically
- Designed for high‚Äëconsumption or burst workloads

#### Traffic Assignment Modes

**Manual Hydration**

- Traffic is assigned manually from the pool to a VPS

**Auto‚ÄëHydration**

- System automatically assigns traffic increments (e.g. 100‚ÄØGB)
- Triggered when a VPS reaches ~99% of its included traffic
- Requires sufficient pool balance
